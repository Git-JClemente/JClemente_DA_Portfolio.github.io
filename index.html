HR Analytics of Hiring Data Scientists

The data of participants to a training course offered by a company interested in hiring data scientists is analyzed to predict the factors that lead to interest in seeking new employment with that company. This project is performed using R.

U.S. vs. Global COVID-19 Dashboard

Using SQL and Tableau, I developed a dashboard that provides a comparative look at the global and U.S. Covid-19 statistics (as of 8/8/2021) as well as a timeline of the spread of cases in the U.S. with respect to some relevant key events.

Bellabeat Wearable Health Smart Devices

Fitbit usage data was analyzed in order to uncover insights to help Bellabeat marketing team to strategize & explore potential growth opportunities in the fitness tracking device industry. R was used to prepare, process, clean, analyze and visualize the data.
